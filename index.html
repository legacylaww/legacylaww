About Gagan Anand

Gagan Anand brings over three decades of experience in project development, public-private partnerships, construction law, FIDIC-based contracts, international arbitration and complex dispute resolution.

As the Managing Partner of Legacy Law Offices LLP, he has advised governments, multilateral institutions and private sector clients on high-value infrastructure, energy and transport projects across multiple jurisdictions.

For more details, visit his official profile here: https://www.legacylawoffices.com/gagan-anand

Key Expertise

Infrastructure, Energy & Transport

He advises on major infrastructure, energy and transport projects, including metro systems, high-speed rail, dedicated freight corridors, urban transport and large-scale real estate developments.

PPP & Project Structuring

His work includes public-private partnerships, concession agreements, procurement structuring, policy advisory and development of bankable project frameworks.

Construction Law & FIDIC

Gagan Anand has deep experience in FIDIC-based contracts, EPC frameworks, construction claims, risk allocation, contract drafting and dispute avoidance.

International Arbitration

He is known for his expertise in construction arbitration, infrastructure disputes, complex claims management and dispute resolution strategies.

Policy & Regulatory Frameworks

He has contributed to PPP policies, model concession agreements, regulatory frameworks and state-level infrastructure development initiatives.

Sustainability & Digital Laws

His thought leadership also covers climate-resilient infrastructure, sustainability in construction, digital personal data protection and legal policy developments.

Professional Experience & Work Highlights

Gagan Anand has advised on complex and high-value infrastructure, energy and transport projects across Asia, the Middle East, Europe and Africa. His work covers project structuring, procurement, bid process management, contract negotiation, risk mitigation and contract management.

He has served as Chief Legal Advisor at the Punjab Infrastructure Development Board, where he played a key role in PPP policies, model concession agreements and regulatory frameworks. He has also been associated with infrastructure advisory work involving the Himachal Pradesh Infrastructure Development Board.
